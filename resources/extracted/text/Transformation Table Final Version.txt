Transformation Table
(Transcribed from resources/pdf/appendices/Transformation Table Final Version.pdf)

Core Terms

Transformation Level
Your Transformation Level is the number from 0-10 that shows how far your blood has
shifted. When you gain 1 Transformation Level, increase your Transformation Level by 1,
then roll on the Transformation Table using your new Transformation Level.

Transformation
A Transformation is a mutation result gained from the Transformation Table, such as
Dreadblood Eyes, Dreadblood Ears, Blood Fangs, or Mutated Arm. These are active
transformations and remain until a rule says you lose them.

Transformation Table Result
A result on the Transformation Table is not always a Transformation. Nothing Happens is
not a Transformation. Blood Lust is a compulsion result, not a Transformation. Lost is a
special catastrophic result that counts as a Transformation.

Reducing Transformation Level
Your Transformation Level is reduced only when a rule, effect, rest, or condition specifically
says so.

Short Rest. When you finish a Short Rest, reduce your Transformation Level by 1 and lose
all active Transformations. During the same Short Rest, you may make a DC 13 Constitution
(Grit) check. On a success, reduce your Transformation Level by 1 additional level.

Long Rest. When you finish a Long Rest, reduce your Transformation Level to 0 and lose all
active Transformations.

Unconscious. The first time you gain the Unconscious condition after gaining a
Transformation, reduce your Transformation Level by 2 and lose all active Transformations.
You cannot reduce your Transformation Level this way again until you finish a Short or Long
Rest, or until you gain another Transformation.

Result Texts

Blood Lust.
Drink another Blood Vial or suffer 3 Madness.

Mutated Arm.
Suffer 2 madness. Add +1d12 to your damage roll and 5 ft to your range if melee damage.
Suffer 1 madness if you do.

Blood Fangs.
As an melee attack action you can choose to bite a creature within 5 ft, on hit dealing 4d3
piercing damage. You heal the same amount as damage dealt. Suffer 1 madness if you do.

Dreadblood Eyes.
As an action you can gain Blindsight for 10 rounds. Suffer 1 Madness if you do.

Dreadblood Ears.
Your Wisdom (Perception) modifier increases by +5. You may suffer 1 Madness to make a
Wisdom (Perception) check with Advantage.

Nothing Happens.

Lost.
(Special catastrophic result; counts as a Transformation. Effects are DM-secret.)

The Table
Roll 1d20 against your (new) Transformation Level (columns 1-10).
