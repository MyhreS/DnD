ARMOR OF AGATHYS
Level 1

Type: Protection Rite
Performing: Bonus Action
Range: Self
Duration: 20 rounds

You call upon a Patron of frozen ruin, wreathing your body in otherworldly frost. You gain 5
Temporary Hit Points.
If a creature hits you with a melee attack while you have these Temporary Hit Points, that creature
takes 5 Cold damage. This Rite ends early if you have no Temporary Hit Points remaining.
Using Higher-Level Strain. The Temporary Hit Points and Cold damage both increase by 5 for
each Strain level above 1.




FORESIGHT
Level 9

Type: Protection Rite
Performing: Three Actions
Range: Touch
Duration: 10 rounds

You call upon a Patron that peers beyond the veil of time and fate. By its guidance, a willing
creature you touch is granted eeting glimpses of the immediate future, allowing it to perceive
danger moments before it occurs.
For the duration, the target has Advantage on all D20 Tests, and other creatures have
Disadvantage on attack rolls against it.
This Rite ends early if you perform it again.
