     THIRD HAND
     Whisper

     Type: Summoning Rite
     Performing: Action
     Range: 30 feet
     Duration: Concentration, 10 rounds

     A spectral, oating hand not from this world appears at a point you choose within range. The
     hand lasts for the duration. The hand vanishes if it is ever more than 30 feet away from you or if
     you perform this rite again. When you perform the rite, you can use the hand to manipulate an
     object, open an unlocked door or container, stow or retrieve an item from an open container, or
     pour the contents out of a vial.
     As a Rite action on your later turns, you can control the hand thus again. As part of that action,
     you can move the hand up to 30 feet.
     The hand can't attack, or carry more than 10 pounds.

     CALL STARBORN HORROR
     Level 9

     Type: Summoning Rite
     Performing: Action
     Range: 120 feet
     Duration: 5 rounds

     You tear open reality and call forth a true Starborn Horror, a servant of the outer dark that was
     never meant to enter the mortal world.
     When the Starborn Horror manifests, each creature within a 30-foot-radius Sphere centered on a
     point within range must make a Wisdom saving throw.
     On a failed save, a creature takes 10d10 Mind damage, su ers 6 Madness, and gains the
     Frightened condition for the duration.
     On a successful save, a creature takes half as much damage and su ers 2 Madness.
     Each other creature that can see or hear the Starborn Horror su ers 2 Madness.
     You su er 6 Madness when you perform this Rite.

     The Starborn Horror uses the Starborn Horror stat block. It is not friendly to you or your allies, nor

     action it takes. When determining targets, use your Madness die system.
     The Starborn Horror disappears when the Rite ends.
