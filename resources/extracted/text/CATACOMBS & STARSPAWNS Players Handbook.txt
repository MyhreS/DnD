By default your character has unlimited
insignificant item slots and 3 significant
item slots. You also have your hands
which in addition can carry a total of
either 2 significant items or 1 oversized
item. This represent items carried at your
front, back, hip as well as what you are
actively holding in your hands. Keep in
mind that holding something in your hand
at all times prevents your hands to be free
to do other interactions. You can add slots
for significant items by equipping a
backpack, bandolier or tool belt.
