MINDCRACK
Whisper

Type: Mind In uence Rite
Performing: Action
Range: 60 feet
Duration: 1 round

You try to temporarily break the mind of one creature you can see within range, opening their
minds to the words of your patron through whispers. The target must succeed on an Intelligence
saving throw or take 1d6 Mind damage and subtract 1d4 from the next saving throw it makes
before the end of your next turn.
Whisper Upgrade. The damage increases by 1d6 when you reach levels 5 (2d6), 11 (3d6), and 17
(4d6).


MINDGRAB
Level 2

Type: Mind In uence Rite
Performing: Action
Range: 120 feet
Duration: Concentration

By your words a patron will attach itself onto the mind of one creature you can see within range.
The target makes a Wisdom saving throw, taking 3d8 Mind damage on a failed save or half as
much damage on a successful one. On a failed save, you also always know the target's location
until the spell ends, but only while the two of you are on the same plane of existence. While you
have this knowledge, the target can't become hidden from you, and if it has the Invisible
condition, it gains no bene t from that condition against you.
Using a Higher-Level Strain. The damage increases by 1d8 for each Strain level above 2.
