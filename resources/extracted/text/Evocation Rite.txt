ELDRITCH BLAST
Whisper

Type: Evocation Rite
Performing: Action
Range: 120 feet
Duration: Instantaneous

You hurl a beam of crackling energy. Make a ranged spell attack against one creature or object in
range.
On a hit, the target takes 1d10 Eldritch Power damage.
Whisper Upgrade. The spell creates two beams at level 5, three beams at level 11, and four
beams at level 17. You can direct the beams at the same target or at di erent ones. Make a
separate attack roll for each beam.



ELDRITCH STRIKE
Whisper

Type: Evocation Rite
Performing: Action
Range: Self
Duration: Instantaneous
Special Requirements: A weapon with which you have pro ciency

Guided by your Patron's will, you make one attack with the weapon used to perform this Rite.
The attack uses your Rite Performing ability for the attack and damage rolls instead of using
Strength or Dexterity. If the attack deals damage, it can be Eldrich Power damage or the
weapon's normal damage type (your choice).
Whisper Upgrade. Whether you deal Eldrich Power damage or the weapon's normal damage
type, the attack deals extra Eldrich Power damage when you reach levels 5 (1d6), 11 (2d6), and 17
(3d6).



ELDRITCH LIGHTNING
Whisper

Type: Evocation Rite
Performing: Action
Range: Self
Duration: Instantaneous
You call upon a Patron, letting a fragment of its voice burst from your body as crackling lightning.
Each creature in a 5-foot Emanation originating from you must succeed on a Constitution saving
throw or take 1d6 Lightning damage. The Rite also makes a thunderous sound can be heard up to
100 feet away.
Whisper Upgrade. The damage increases by 1d6 when you reach levels 5 (2d6), 11 (3d6), and 17
(4d6).

ELDRITCH CHAIN OF BOLTS
Level 1

Type: Evocation Rite
Performing: Action
Range: 60 feet
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 rounds

You invoke a Patron of storm and ruin, causing a beam of crackling lightning to lance toward a
creature within range.
Make a ranged Rite attack against the target. On a hit, the target takes 2d12 Lightning damage.
Until the Rite ends, you can take a Bonus Action on each of your turns to cause the lightning to
surge through the target, dealing 1d12 Lightning damage automatically.
The Rite ends if the target is ever outside the Rite's range or has Total Cover from you.
Using Higher-Level Strain. The initial damage increases by 1d12 for each Strain level above 1.
