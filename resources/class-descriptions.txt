HUNTER BRUTE
The first hunters learned quickly that silvered words and clever plans mean little when a Dreadblood breaks the line. Brutes are the iron answer to that horror: scarred front-line killers who meet monstrosity with muscle, rage, and the stubborn refusal to die.

HUNTER SCOUT
Before rifles became the hunter’s sacred thunder, Scouts were the first to prove that a Dreadblood could be studied, stalked, and dropped from afar. They are pathfinders, marksmen, and survivalists who read the land like scripture and kill before the dark can close its jaws.

HUNTER WARDEN
Wardens are the steady hand in a world gone mad. Trusted more than most hunters, they command bands through discipline, tactics, and cold battlefield judgment, turning frightened killers into something sharper than fear.

HUNTER STALKER
When brute force and rifle fire were not enough, the bands turned to assassins, cutthroats, and thieves. Stalkers are the knives in the blind corner: silent murderers who learned that some horrors must be killed before they know the hunt has begun.

HUNTER BLOODBOUND
Some hunters curse the Bloodbound as fanatics, and perhaps they are right. These hunters drink from the edge of damnation, forcing Dreadblood transformations through their own flesh for a few terrible moments of power, always one heartbeat from becoming the very thing they hunt.

HUNTER DEEPCALLER
Every Deepcaller carries a Book of the Deepcaller, though no one knows who wrote the first, or why copies find their way to those willing to listen. Feared as lunatics and tolerated only for the impossible power they bring, Deepcallers open their minds to the things beneath thought—and sometimes, those things answer.
