`art-src/creatures/`) are derived from **[game-icons.net](https://game-icons.net)**
icons, licensed under **[CC BY 3.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/3.0/)**.
We recolour them per creature but the silhouettes are the artists' work.
